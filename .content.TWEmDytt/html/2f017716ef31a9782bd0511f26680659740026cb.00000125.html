About BU books

Cost:   Absolutely free.
 
What:   We match buyers and sellers, removing the middle-man.
 
Why:   So you save $$! We're college students, and we're sick of being ripped off by monopolistic university bookstores.
 
Who:   Anyone can use BUbooks, though we created the site primarily for college students and book aficionados in the greater Boston area.
 
How:   Posting a book for sale is easy - simply visit the Post page. If you find a book you like, click on it and you'll be given the opportunity to contact the seller. Potential buyer and seller correspond privately via email or phone to work out a price and mode of exchange. Meeting at a cafe during daytime is common, but mail also works. We take no part in the exchange; BUbooks does not sell or buy books - it simply facilitates the process.
 
Our Dream:   To free students from the tyranny of used textbook monopolies. To put smiles on the face of all those who love book bargains. To bring the greater Boston populace together in moments of shared literary-minded euphoria. (We're Durkheimian at heart.)
 
We encourage you to try a Search.
Or a leisurely Browse.
Or, if you're strapped for cash, Post a book for sale.

Enjoy!
