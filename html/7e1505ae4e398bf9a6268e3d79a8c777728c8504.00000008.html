About Us

BUBooks.com was founded by a Baylor Junior ('05) and a Junior at Stanford University ('05) who wanted a better way to buy and sell used textbooks. We set up BUBooks.com (Buy Used Books.com) to help out our fellow students with this common problem. We provide this free service to bring together potential buyers and sellers of used textbooks at Baylor and to offer an alternative to high campus bookstore prices.

BUBooks also runs a local professor rating service to help Baylor students pick the right professor. Currently, we have 4419 professor ratings, and 720 Baylor professors rated.

BUBooks uses banner ads from local businesses to keep the service free for students. Although BUBooks will obtain revenue from these ads, it is still not for profit. All proceeds beyond our operating expenses are used to improve the service and availability of the website.

As always, we would appreciate it if you would contact us if you have ANY problem with the website. Once the problem is known, we can quickly fix it.
