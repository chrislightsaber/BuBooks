Frequently Asked Questions

Q - How do I create an account?
A - On the BUbooks.com homepage, simply look for the link that says "Not a member? Create an account here," and follow the instructions.

Q - Do I have to be a member to search for a book?
A - You must be a member to get the seller's information after searching. This is only to maintain the privacy of our users. Creating an account takes only about thirty seconds.

Q - Why can't I log in?
A - BUBooks.com requires cookies be enabled in your browser for session management.

Q - How do I add a book to the BUbooks.com database?
A - First, sign in at the BUbooks.com homepage. From there you will be brought to your personal homepage where there is a link to add your books.

Q - How do I change my account information if I move?
A - After signing in, there is a link in the top right of your homepage that says "Update/ Change My account information." Simply click on the link and fill in the info.

Q - Am I responsible for deleting my sold books?
A - YES! If you don't you will continue getting inquiries about the book after you have sold it.

Q - How do I search for a book other than by TITLE?
A - On the search engine next to title there is a downward facing arrow. Press on it to get the options of ISBN, Author, or Course.

Q - What is an ISBN number?
A - An ISBN is a 10 digit number printed on the back of ALL Textbooks. Searching by ISBN is the fastest and most reliable way to search because every edition of a book has its own ISBN.

Profrate

Q - How do I rate a professor?
A - At ProfRate, take a look at the list of all the professors already listed. If your professor is listed, click on his/her name then click on the link saying Add a Rating. If your Professor is not on the list, click on the link Create a New Professor Rating Entry and post!

Q - What does Narrow By Department do?
A - Narrowing by department will show ONLY the professors listed in the specific field chosen.

Q - How do I add a rating to an existing professor?
A - Go to the professor's rating page and in the Green title bar over users individual comments, there is a Yellow link that says "Add a rating". Click on this.
