Comments:2303 - Ms. Millar is actually not as bad as it seems. She's very kind and will help you if you ask. She gave us quite a bit of extra credit, which the other professors didn't (from what I heard). If you do the reading quizzes on canvas and get a 100 on all of them she gives 4-5 points ON THE EXAM. The reading quizzes are VERY easy! They aren't timed and you have two tries to do them (you can literally look at the answers after the first try, right them down, and fill them in for the second try since the questions are the same). Along with that, we had about 3 quizzes (can't remember exactly) that covered lecture/HW. The HW is usually 5 questions, which can take a while depending on how well you know the material right after class. It helps to review what you're going to do in class that day before class starts so you have a better understanding at the end of class. Also, you have two attempts to do the HW (which is plenty) and she drops two of them at the end. The tests cover lectures/notes and the HW assignments. She posts fill in the blank notes with accounting concepts and examples where you solve the problems out. The first test we took was all multiple choice and then for the next two we had multiple choice and solve-it-out problems. The final exam was all multiple choice (this was the easiest test out of them all, in my opinion) and was comprehensive. The final exam can replace your lowest test grade, which is awesome (and got me the A that I wanted desperately)! ***TIP: I didn't realize this until after the second test, but it REALLY helps to do extra practice problems on My Accounting Lab. To study for the tests, you should do as many practice problems as you can and do the the hard ones too. The more you practice, the better you get at them. Practice REALLY does make perfect for this course (with any professor). You won't see anything on the tests that hasn't been covered in the lectures/notes or HW assignments. Also, DO NOT neglect reviewing the basic concepts for the test because these are the really easy questions you shouldn't be getting wrong. For extra help, I recommend going to SI because you'll get extra practice worksheets and will have the opportunity to understand the material you previously weren't clear on or didn't understand at all.  - Grade In Class:A
