Comments:Econometrics - Dr. Ward is a great professor for Econometrics! He explains things well, gives 6 homework sets ("problem sets") throughout the semester, and will make time to meet with you outside of office hours if you need. He is a very nice man and has sound effects (like dot dot dot dot dot when dotting his lines) when drawing on the board which can be quite entertaining. He is very passionate about Econometrics as well. I really enjoyed how he always shared the results of recent studies in the news. Also, there is one project in this class which is doing "research" and writing a paper (10 pages long), but it is split out across the semester so it is not that bad. The project also is really interesting because you get to choose your topic. He previously taught in Australia for a few years, which is so cool! Most people, who stuck it out in this class (i.e. didn't drop) and worked hard, got at least a B. Highly recommend Dr. Ward for Econometrics!   - Grade In Class:A
